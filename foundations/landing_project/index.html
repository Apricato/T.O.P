Species we preservate

The sicklefin lemon shark is a species of requiem shark, known for its distinctive sickle-shaped dorsal fin. It is found in the tropical waters of the Indo-Pacific region.

The puffadder shyshark is a small, elusive species of catshark found in the coastal waters of South Africa. It is known for its unique defensive behavior of curling into a ring when threatened.

The scalloped hammerhead is a species of hammerhead shark, known for its distinctive scalloped edges on its head. It is found in tropical and warm temperate waters worldwide.

The silvertip shark is a large species of requiem shark, known for its distinctive white-tipped dorsal fin. It is found in the tropical waters of the Indian and Pacific Oceans.
